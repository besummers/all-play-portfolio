# Risk Register – Incident Intelligence Dashboard

| Risk | Category | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|----------|-------------|--------|---------------------|
| Limited access to real incident data | Data | Medium | High | Use synthetic datasets and anonymized logs to prototype functionality; validate structure early. |
| Model accuracy variability | Technical | Medium | Medium | Implement validation checks, fallback logic, and iterative tuning based on test datasets. |
| Schedule compression due to academic deadlines | Schedule | High | Medium | Front‑load high‑risk tasks, add buffer time, and prioritize critical path items. |
| Security concerns around handling sensitive data | Security | Low | High | Enforce strict data handling rules, local‑only processing, and no storage of sensitive information. |
| Scope expansion as new features emerge | Scope | Medium | Medium | Maintain a strict MVP definition and evaluate new ideas through a change‑control lens. |
